IMAGE TIPS:
-----------
• All images should be in JPG format
• Optimize images for web (compress to keep file size under 300KB each)
• Use high-quality photos that showcase the products clearly
• Ensure good lighting and professional photography
• Color palette should match: soft pink, peach, gold tones
• All product photos should have clean backgrounds or lifestyle settings
• Gallery images can be lifestyle shots, detail shots, or product showcases
